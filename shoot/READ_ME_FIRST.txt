VILO LIVE ROOM - SIMPLE SHOOT BUILD

MAC
1. Double-click START_VILO.command.
2. In the browser, choose 1, 2, 4, or 9.
3. Press START WITH ACTRESS CAMERA.
4. Leave it alone. The coworker clips change by themselves.

WINDOWS
Double-click START_VILO_WINDOWS.bat and follow the same three browser steps.

DIRECTOR EMERGENCY CONTROLS
- Tap the tiny upper-left corner or press D.
- 1 / 2 / 4 / 9 changes the shot.
- Space gives everyone fresh reactions.
- H hides every control.
- F enters full screen.
- R starts/stops browser screen recording where supported.

The HTML contains every supplied coworker video. It needs no clip folder and no internet connection.
